Static Demos

Here I show 4 examples of the BBC Pagination Device being used to display news feeds of different sizes to demonstrate how the component copes with standard and truncated pagination as per the BBC GEL Pattern behaviours, depending upon the size of the item set being rendered. The data source for these examples is from static JSON files.

Average Feed - 12 items

A typically sized feed, showing the full range of behaviours as per the GEL Pattern for pagination. Cycle through the news feed to see the left ellipses appear when there are 2 hidden items, how the active page link stays in the center and then how the right ellipses disappear as you near the end of the paginated set.

JSON Test Data - news-12.json

Small Feed - 7 items

A feed of exactly 7 news items, to demonstrate the standard pagination GEL Pattern behaviour without any ellipses.

Large Feed - 35 items

A much larger feed to demonstrate that the pagination device can support large data sets.
