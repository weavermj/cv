Live Demos

Here I show examples of the BBC Pagination Device being used with live JSON feeds from various BBC news tickers.

BBC Sport Ticker Feed

Here the BBC Sport news items from the ticker are displayed with the title showing, to clearly identify which area of sport each article is about.

JSON Data (BBC Sport)

BBC News Ticker Feed

When showing just BBC news items, the user experience is best when the title is hidden as most items seem to have a prompt of "LATEST" in the JSON data. As "LATEST" doesn't really add any information to the context of the feed, hiding the title saves screen real-estate.
