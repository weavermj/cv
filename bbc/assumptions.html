Assumptions

To comply with both the GEL Pattern for pagination and the requirements of the interview task, I have made the following assumptions:

Number of buttons

The interview task stipulated that the pagination device should contain no more than 7 buttons. I have interpreted this as no more than 7 page buttons, so the previous and next navigation links do not count as buttons in my implementation. This allows the user to navigate more easily between the previous and next items using the page number links either side of the active page link (when the active page link is in the centre of the pagination options), rather than having to move their pointing device to the far left or right of the component.

Ellipses when truncating

The GEL Pattern for pagination states not to use ellipses when there are less than 2 hidden pages. I have interpreted this to mean that when there are less than 2 hidden pages at just one end of the pagination component, then do not show the ellipses for that end of the component.
